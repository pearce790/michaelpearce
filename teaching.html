Undergraduate Statistics Directed Reading Program
In addition to my more formal experiences, I've led a few projects through the UW Statistics Directed Reading Program (DRP). These one-on-one experiences are intended to help undergraduates from a wide variety of backgrounds gain exposure to statistical topics not often covered in the curriculum and learn about life as a graduate student or researcher.
