Undergraduate Statistics Directed Reading Program
In addition to my more formal experiences as a teaching assistant, I've led two projects with undergraduate students as part of the UW Statistics Directed Reading Program (DRP). The one-on-one experiences are intended to help undergraduates from a wide variety of backgrounds gain exposure to areas of statistics not often covered in the curriculum and learn about life as a graduate student and researcher.
